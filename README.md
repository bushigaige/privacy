This Privacy Policy describes how NoteAdd (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application.

We may collect the following types of information:

The App may offer auto-renewable subscriptions.

We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are processed by Apple.

We use your information solely to:

We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

	•	All user-generated content is stored locally on your device.
	•	We do not upload your personal data to external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
